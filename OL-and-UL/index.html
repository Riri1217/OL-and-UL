Jorielyn's Recipe for Delicious Bread Rolls

Ingredients

For the batter and breading:

  • 1 loaf Bread (large size)
  • 1/2 kg. Flour
  • 2 pcs. Medium Eggs
  • 1 tsp. Salt
  • 1 tsp. Pepper
  • 1 tsp. Yeast
  • 1/2 kg. Bread Crumbs
  • 1 kg. Cooking Oil
  • Water
  • Toothpick (optional)

For the filling:

  • 10 pcs. Jumbo Hotdog
  • Any kind of Cheese

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour with water and add the eggs, yeast, salt, and pepper, then mix until well combined.
  2. Flatten the slices of bread.
  3. Add 1 hotdog and cheese on each bread the roll it (you can use toothpick to lock the bread).
  4. Dip the bread with hotdog and cheese into the batter.
  5. Then cover the coated bread with bread crumbs.
  6. On a pan with with the hot cooking oil, fry the bread roll until golden brown.
  7. Once golden brown, get it from the pan.
  8. Drain the oil and remove the toothpick from the bread.
  9. Serve and enjoy.
